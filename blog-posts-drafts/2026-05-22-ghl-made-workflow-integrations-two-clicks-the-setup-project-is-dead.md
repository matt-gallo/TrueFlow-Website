On May 21, 2026, GoHighLevel shipped a **common integration experience** for Workflow Automations. Integration-powered actions and triggers now show required fields upfront. If the integration isn't connected, the panel opens with a locked-state preview and a **Connect your account** banner that fires the OAuth in a new tab — you finish wiring up Slack or Stripe, switch back, and the fields unlock. Multiple connected accounts are supported per integration, so one workflow can write to your client subaccount and your master subaccount without rebuilding the step.

That paragraph used to be a $3,000 line item on an "AI integration scoping" SOW. As of yesterday, it is a button.

## What Just Changed (In English)

Wiring a third-party tool into a GHL workflow used to be a four-step dance: discovery call to inventory what the workflow needs, separate setup session for the OAuth, follow-up to debug which fields were required, then a billable hour to extend the build because nobody remembered which Stripe account production should use. Agencies have quoted all four steps as a "Phase 1 Integration Audit" for two years.

GHL just collapsed those four steps into the workflow builder itself. There is no audit left to do.
